I am an undergrad student at NTUA School of Architecture. This is my website for Creative Arts and Sciences, where you could search and study my geometric ideas that can be based on ready-made software tools - Powerpoint and Word - and tools that I create myself through the programming languages Javascript and p5 (which is an extension of Javascript). My aim is to materialize, clarify and concretize with the right means my abstract ideas and concepts through creativity, research and innovation. Finally, I desire to develop myself by combining my talents and collaborating with fellow students. Here you can have a look at my projects.
